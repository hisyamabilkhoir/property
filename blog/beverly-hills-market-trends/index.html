Beverly Hills Market Analysis: Q1 Resilience

By Hisyam Abilkhoir March 02, 2024 5 min read

The first quarter of 2024 has shown that the Beverly Hills ultra-luxury market remains remarkably resilient despite broader economic volatility. As investors seek "safe havens," the 90210 zip code continues to perform at record levels.

1. Inventory and Demand

We are seeing a historic low in available inventory for properties priced above $20 million. This creates a high-stakes environment where off-market deals are becoming the standard for the most prestigious estates.

2. Modern Architectural Domination

While the classic Mediterranean style still holds allure, modern architectural masterpieces with comprehensive wellness facilities and high-tech security are fetching the highest price per square foot in today's market.

3. International Capital Flow

We've observed a significant uptick in interest from European and Asian investors seeking to diversify their portfolios with prime California assets. This global demand is the primary driver behind the current market stability.
